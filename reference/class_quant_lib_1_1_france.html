Detailed Description

French calendars.

  • Saturdays
  • Sundays
  • New Year's Day, January 1st
  • Easter Monday
  • Labour Day, May 1st
  • Armistice 1945, May 8th
  • Ascension, May 10th
  • Pentecôte, May 21st
  • Fête nationale, July 14th
  • Assumption, August 15th
  • All Saint's Day, November 1st
  • Armistice 1918, November 11th
  • Christmas Day, December 25th
