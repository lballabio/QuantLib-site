Detailed Description

Singapore calendars

Holidays for the Singapore exchange (data from http://www.sgx.com/wps/portal/sgxweb/home/trading/securities/trading_hours_calendar):

  • Saturdays
  • Sundays
  • New Year's day, January 1st
  • Good Friday
  • Labour Day, May 1st
  • National Day, August 9th
  • Christmas, December 25th

Other holidays for which no rule is given (data available for 2004-2010, 2012-2014, 2019-2024 only:)

  • Chinese New Year
  • Hari Raya Haji
  • Vesak Poya Day
  • Deepavali
  • Diwali
  • Hari Raya Puasa
